AI Chat Exporter Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-17

This Privacy Policy describes how SAVO ("we", "us") handles information when you use the AI Chat Exporter browser extension and our website.

1) Summary (plain English)

  • The extension is designed to export chat content locally in your browser.
  • We do not store your chat content on our servers.
  • Payments (if you buy a plan) are handled by our payment provider (e.g., Lemon Squeezy and its payment processors). We do not store your card details.

2) What data the extension processes

When you click an export action, the extension may read content from the currently active tab to extract the conversation you chose to export. This processing happens locally on your device to generate the export.

  • Chat content: processed locally to create the export.
  • Clipboard / downloads: if you click "Copy" or export to a file, the data is written to your clipboard or downloaded to your computer.
  • Extension settings: we may store non-sensitive preferences (e.g., export options) in browser storage.

3) Permissions (why we need them)

  • activeTab / host access: to read the visible chat page only when you trigger export.
  • scripting: to run content scripts needed to extract/format the conversation after you initiate export.
  • clipboardWrite: to copy formatted exports when you click "Copy".
  • downloads: to save exported files to your device when you choose to download.
  • storage: to store your non-sensitive settings locally.

4) Payments (if you purchase)

If you purchase a paid plan, checkout and payment processing are handled by our payment provider. We may receive limited purchase-related information such as your email address, order status, and entitlement/license status to unlock paid features.

We do not receive or store your full payment card number.

5) Analytics (optional)

We may collect anonymous or aggregated usage analytics to improve reliability and user experience. We do not use analytics to read or store your chat content.

6) Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate the product (for example, payment providers and infrastructure providers).

7) Data retention

  • Chat content: not stored by us; it remains on your device unless you export/save it.
  • Support communications: if you email us, we may keep those messages to help you.
  • Purchase records: retained as required for accounting/tax/refund compliance by the payment provider and/or us.

8) Your choices

  • You can uninstall the extension at any time from your browser.
  • You can delete exported files from your device.
  • For account/purchase questions, contact us and we will help.

9) Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: support@getsavo.app
